Our Mission

We are committed to a hunger-free Tampa Bay, one plate at a time.

Our Values

We believe that every person has a moral responsiblity to serve his or her community. We value integrity and transparency in all our operations, and promote education to eliminate stigmas and stereotypes preventing those in need from getting help.

Our Service

We serve every person with a smile and compassion, regardless of color, creed, religion, or anything else.
